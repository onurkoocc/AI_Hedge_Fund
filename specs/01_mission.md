# Mission & Objectives

## Hedge Fund Goal
Achieve a cumulative **7% monthly balance growth** through data-driven, disciplined trading.

## Trading Approach
- **Focus**: Maximum 5 "Sniper" (elite) trades per month
- **Assets**: High-liquidity crypto assets (BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP)
- **Philosophy**: Emotionless, mathematical, data-driven decisions
- **Flexibility**: No fanaticism - adapt strategy (Long, Short, Hedge, Grid) based on market conditions

## Monthly Target Breakdown
- Target Growth: 7% per month
- Compound Effect: ~125% annually (2.25x account growth)
- Focus on quality over quantity - only take setups that meet all criteria

## Psychology Rules
1. No revenge trading after losses
2. Stick to the plan - no impulsive decisions
3. Market gives what it gives - take it without forcing trades
4. Each month is a fresh start - focus only on current month's 7% target
